Cookie Policy

Last updated: May 2025

This Cookie Policy explains how PrimeTiers uses cookies and similar technologies when you visit our website.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device by your browser. They help websites remember information about your visit, such as login sessions.

2. Cookies We Use

Cookie Name Purpose Type Duration
connect.sid Admin session authentication — keeps admin users logged in Essential 24 hours

3. What We Don't Use

PrimeTiers does not use:

  • Advertising or tracking cookies
  • Analytics cookies (Google Analytics, etc.)
  • Social media tracking pixels
  • Third-party marketing cookies

4. Essential Cookies

The only cookie we set (connect.sid) is strictly necessary for admin panel functionality. It is only set when an admin logs in and is automatically deleted after 24 hours or on logout. Regular visitors to the public site do not receive any cookies.

5. Managing Cookies

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Blocking the session cookie will prevent admin login but will not affect the public-facing tier list, rankings, or player profiles.

  • Firefox
  • Safari

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy if we add new features that require additional cookies. Changes will be posted here with an updated date.

About PrimeTiers

🎯 Our Mission

PrimeTiers exists to bring structure, fairness, and recognition to competitive Minecraft PvP. We believe every skilled player deserves an official rank — tested by certified testers, tracked over time, and visible to the entire community.

Whether you're grinding Crystal PvP, dominating UHC, or mastering the Axe meta — PrimeTiers is where your skill gets recognized.

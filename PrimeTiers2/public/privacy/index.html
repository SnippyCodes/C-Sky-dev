Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2025

PrimeTiers ("we", "us", or "our") operates the PrimeTiers website and tier list platform. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our service.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Minecraft usernames and UUIDs — submitted by administrators for ranking purposes
  • Discord user IDs — optionally linked for tester profiles and online status
  • Usage data — standard server logs including IP addresses, browser type, and pages visited
  • Session data — temporary session cookies for admin authentication

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To display player tier rankings and leaderboards publicly
  • To show tester profiles and online status on the Testers page
  • To authenticate admin users and protect the admin panel
  • To improve site performance and fix bugs

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. Player data (username, UUID, tier rankings) is publicly displayed as part of the tier list service. We use the following third-party services:

  • mc-heads.net / crafty.gg — for Minecraft skin rendering
  • Discord API — for online presence status (requires bot in server)
  • Neon (PostgreSQL) — for database hosting

4. Data Retention

Player and tester data is retained as long as the account is active on the platform. Admins may delete player records at any time through the admin panel. Server logs are retained for up to 30 days.

5. Your Rights

If you wish to have your data removed from PrimeTiers, please contact us on Discord at discord.gg/eDNEpky8Tf. We will process removal requests within 7 days.

6. Cookies

We use session cookies solely for admin authentication. No tracking or advertising cookies are used. See our Cookie Policy for details.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

8. Contact

For privacy-related questions, contact us via Discord: discord.gg/eDNEpky8Tf
